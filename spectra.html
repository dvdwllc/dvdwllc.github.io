What's that cool picture?

The image at the bottom of the screen is a slightly cropped and edited version of the plot shown to the right. Each line is a collection of optical absorbance measurements taken across a wide range of wavelengths of light, and is used track the progress over time of a chemical reaction in solution. In this case, the reaction is the charging of a particular battery material. Yes, you can "see" a battery charging (if you set up the experiment properly)! The amount of time elapsed after the start of the reaction is represented by a change in line color from dark blue to dark red. Click here to see how I created this plot using Python and Matplotlib.
